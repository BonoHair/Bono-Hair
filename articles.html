Essential Hair System Maintenance Tips

Published Nov 10, 2025

Maintaining a natural-looking hair system requires attention to detail and consistent care. Bono Hair provides premium wigs, toupees, toppers, and custom hair systems designed for comfort and realism. Following proper maintenance practices ensures longevity and a flawless appearance.

1. Regular Cleaning

Wash your hair system with gentle, sulfate-free shampoos every 1-2 weeks depending on wear. Avoid harsh rubbing and always condition using lightweight conditioners to prevent tangling and dryness.

2. Proper Storage

When not in use, store your system on a wig stand or mannequin head. Avoid direct sunlight and humid environments to maintain hair quality and prevent base damage.

3. Adhesive and Tape Care

Use adhesives recommended by Bono Hair, and remove residue carefully with approved solvents. Regular replacement of tapes and adhesives ensures secure attachment without harming the system.

4. Styling Practices

Use low-heat tools, and apply heat protectants if styling. Avoid excessive combing or brushing, especially on lace or skin bases, to preserve the structure and appearance.

5. Professional Maintenance

Regular check-ins with trained professionals are recommended for deep cleaning, adjustments, and repairs. This ensures your hair system stays natural, comfortable, and durable over time.

By following these tips, wearers can enjoy a natural-looking, comfortable, and long-lasting hair system. Bono Hair remains committed to providing high-quality products and guidance to help customers maintain their confidence and style.
